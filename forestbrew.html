Summary

Forest Brew is a cozy, story-driven potion crafting game, where players step into the role of a newly graduated druid, tasked with their first real duty. Their magical forest is in danger as villagers, or settlers, are arriving in waves, their minds set on personal gain and profit – only seeing the endless possibilities offered by the magical forest, but not caring about the delicate magical balance. To stop them, players can explore the forest, collect ingredients, craft, and activate various potions.





My Responsibilities
